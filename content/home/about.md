Adrian Du»ôa is a Full Professor at the University of Bucharest, Department of Sociology, where he teaches social statistics, research methodology and measurement in the social sciences. He is a leading QCA scholar (member of the Advisory Board and Steering Committee at COMPASSS), having published extensively on methodological aspects.
